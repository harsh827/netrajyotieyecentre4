Welcome To Netrajyoti Eye Centre

Eye Clinic in Dhanbad – Netrajyoti Eye Centre

Netrajyoti Eye Centre is a leading eye clinic in Dhanbad, Jharkhand, founded by Dr. K.P. Gupta, providing advanced ophthalmology services including cataract surgery, retina care and glaucoma treatment.

Dr. Gupta completed his M.S. from RIMS Ranchi and pursued advanced fellowship training at the prestigious Sankar Nethralaya, Chennai, along with specialized training at AIIMS New Delhi.

With more than 22 years of experience in ophthalmology, the centre is dedicated to providing modern, affordable, and compassionate eye care services for patients of all ages.
